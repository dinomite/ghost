Packing Bricks For The Goruck Challenge

03 December 2012

I have finally signed up for a GORUCK Challenge. The event is 6 months off, but thanks to Jason posting Wrapping Bricks Explained I've been playing around with packing bricks in my rucks.

I followed the instructions on the above mentioned post for wrapping the bricks in pairs and took Asha Wagner's advice for securing the bricks in a GORUCK pack using Sea to Summit 3/4" straps to secure them on the ruck's internal PALS webbing. To the right are two pairs of bricks (4 total) in a GR Echo.

I'm pretty light, just under 150 most days, so 4 bricks is the prescription. A yoga block is the perfect thing to fill the extra spaceâ€”dense but soft and about the size of two bricks. One note is that you can indeed fit 6 bricks into a GR Echo, with just a bit of room to spare. The third package of bricks takes the place of the yoga block, with a bit of space on either side. Unfortunately, there isn't nearly enough space for a hydration bladder along with them. If you're taking 6 bricks to the Challenge, get a GR1 or Radio Ruck.

Drew Stephens
